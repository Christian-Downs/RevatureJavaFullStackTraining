Homemade hemp milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw unchelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 tsp of sea salt
  • 1-2 tsp of maple syrup

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a high speed blender.
  2. Blend on high for about 1-2 minutes
  3. Store in fridge for up to 5 days.
    If you let it sit for more than 5 days, it will go BAD
